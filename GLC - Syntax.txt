NominalSintagma -> Determiner Noun | PersonalPronoun | Noun | PossessivePronoun Noun
VerbalSintagma -> Verb PhraseConstruction
PhraseConstruction -> Preposition Verb PhraseCont | Preposition Noun | PhraseCont

PhraseCont -> AdverbialConstruction  | AdjectiveConstruction | Noun | Empty
AdverbialConstruction -> Adverbio | not Noun | not Adjective | not Verb
AdjectiveConstruction -> Adjective | Adjective Noun | Adjective AdjectiveConstruction
